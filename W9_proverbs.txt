The most famous Chinese proverbs:
0. A team of horses will struggle to chase down a spoken word.
1. If you don’t do stupid things you won’t end up in tragedy.
2. If small holes aren’t fixed, then big holes will bring hardship.
3. It’s better to walk thousands of miles than to read thousands of books.
4. A good name is better than a good face.
5. A bird in the hand is worth than two in the bush.
6. No man is a perfect man; no gold is sufficiently bare.
7. Man cannot be judged by looks; seas cannot be measured by cup.
8. Failure is the mother of success.
9. It just needs hard work to grind an iron rod into a needle.
